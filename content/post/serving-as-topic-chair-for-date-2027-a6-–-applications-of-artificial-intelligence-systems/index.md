I am pleased to share that I will serve as **Topic Chair for A6 – Applications of Artificial Intelligence Systems** at **DATE 2027**.

DATE has been one of the main venues where I have presented and followed research on embedded systems, computer architecture, dependable computing, and design automation over the years. I am therefore very happy to contribute to the community in this new role.

The A6 topic covers an increasingly important intersection between artificial intelligence and system design, including AI-enabled applications, efficient deployment of AI systems, and the use of AI techniques in design, optimization, and automation flows. This direction is also closely connected to my own interests in resource-aware machine learning, embedded and real-time systems, hardware/software co-design, and dependable computing.

I look forward to working with the DATE 2027 organizing team, the A6 co-chair, and the technical programme committee to help build a strong and balanced programme.

More information about DATE 2027 can be found on the official conference website.
